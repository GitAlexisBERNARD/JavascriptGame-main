INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to Samurai Chronicles!

Embark on a journey as a skilled samurai with the mission to defeat waves of enemies across challenging levels.

Objective

  • - Level Mode: Survive successive waves of monsters until reaching a specific number.
  • - Endless Mode: Hold out against an endless horde of monsters; the game concludes when you run out of lives. Aim for the highest score by lasting as long as possible!

Special Move

As you conquer a certain number of enemies, your samurai charges a special move. Activating it extends the attack range, enabling you to eliminate multiple enemies in a single powerful strike.

How to Play

Samurai Chronicles is a side-scrolling action/strategy game, limiting your character's movement along the X-axis (left or right).

  • - Enemies approach from both directions.
  • - To attack, press the corresponding directional arrow when an enemy is within range.
  • - The samurai executes a teleporting close combat strike. Beware of a brief vulnerability during directional changes.
